Samay Lakhani's Bio

My journey into the realm of AI began during the pandemic. As a person who likes to "work smart," I was intrigued in the efficiency to automate manual tasks. I subsequently secured an internship with a Silicon Valley-based AI development agency during the 10th grade. Since then, I designed and taught a summer course to educate teenagers in AI, which I had run 3 years.

I worked at various companies, including building client projects for unicorns like Revel Mopeds. However, my real passion lies in creating groundbreaking AI models on the R&D side. This includes state-of-the-art computer vision models for detecting distracted driving and contributing to open-source AI research like LAION.AI. My research has been published, and won competitions, including competing at the Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair.

I was researching brain-computer interfaces to control modular robots as a student at Columbia University. Currently I'm working remotely as an R&D engineer at a health insurance startup in California.

2024. Samay Lakhani.
